

	# threat hunting is the practice of proactively searching for cyber threats that are lurking undetected in a network. 
	cyber threat hunting digs deep to find malicious actors in your environment that have slipped past your initial endpoint security defenses.
	
	Threat hunting tactics, techniques and procedures (TTP) is a technique of threat hunting.
